'''The Ones Who Sleep in the Forest of Light'''
* Having the gacha princes on your team will earn you more points.
** [[Riel]]: 50%
** [[Azalee (Forest)]]: 25%
* This event introduces poison beads (normal beads marked at the bottom right).
** When poison beads are erased, damage will be dealt at the end of the puzzle.
** The damage to be dealt is shown on the HP gauge in red and can be offset by heart pieces.
* 2x times: 12:00 to 13:59, 20:00 to 23:00 JST
* Enemy colours:
** Stage 1 - 4: Green
** Stage 5 - 8: Purple & Green
* There will be a sudden quest 2-3 times daily where more points can be obtained. 2x time bonuses will not apply.
